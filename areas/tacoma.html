Tacoma HVAC planning

Repair, replacement and upgrade options for Tacoma homes.

Tacoma homes can have a mix of older furnaces, aging central AC, ductwork restrictions, additions and comfort issues that change from room to room. A good HVAC recommendation should look at the whole system instead of only the outdoor unit or furnace cabinet.

Apex Service Group supports Tacoma homeowners with furnace repair, AC diagnostics, heat pump installation, mini-split planning and ductwork changes. The goal is to explain what failed, what can be repaired, what replacement includes and whether an efficient heat pump or ductless option makes sense for the home.

  • No-heat and no-cooling service calls
  • Furnace replacement and heat pump upgrade planning
  • Central AC repair, replacement and seasonal service
  • Ductless mini-splits for targeted comfort problems
  • Airflow review, startup testing and clean job closeout
