Yelm ductwork FAQ

Quick answers before requesting service.

Can ductwork make new equipment perform badly?

Yes. Undersized, leaky or restricted ducts can reduce comfort and efficiency even with good equipment.

When is the best time to review ducts?

Ductwork is easiest to address during heat pump, furnace or AC replacement planning.

Do all rooms need new ducts?

Not always. Sometimes targeted return-air or supply modifications are enough to improve comfort.
