Yelm mini-split FAQ

Quick answers before requesting service.

Can one mini-split serve the whole home?

Sometimes, but many homes need multiple indoor heads or a different system type. Layout, insulation and comfort goals decide the right answer.

Where can indoor heads go?

Placement depends on room shape, usable wall area, furniture, drain routing and how air needs to move through the space.

Can I check financing first?

Yes. Payment options can be reviewed before choosing the final project scope.
