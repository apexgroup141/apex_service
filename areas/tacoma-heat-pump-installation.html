Tacoma heat pump FAQ

Quick answers before requesting an estimate.

Can a heat pump replace both AC and heat?

Often, yes. A heat pump can provide efficient cooling and heating, but the right setup depends on ducts, electrical capacity, insulation and backup heat needs.

Do you check ductwork?

Yes. Airflow issues can make new equipment underperform, so duct size, return air and restrictions should be reviewed before final equipment selection.

Can rebates or financing apply?

Eligible projects may qualify for utility incentives, and payment options can be reviewed through Acorn Finance before approving the project.
