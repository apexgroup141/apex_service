Sumner air conditioner FAQ

Quick answers before requesting service.

Why is my AC running but not cooling?

Possible causes include airflow restrictions, dirty coils, refrigerant issues, electrical parts or equipment age.

Should I replace AC with a heat pump?

Sometimes. A heat pump can provide heating and cooling, but the best option depends on the home and existing system.

Do you check airflow?

Yes. Airflow problems can mimic equipment failure and should be reviewed before replacing equipment.
