Bellevue mini-split FAQ

Quick answers before requesting a ductless estimate.

Can one mini-split serve the whole home?

Sometimes, but many homes need multiple indoor heads or a different system type. The right answer depends on layout, insulation, airflow and comfort goals.

Where can indoor heads go?

Placement depends on room shape, usable wall area, furniture, drain routing and how air needs to move through the space.

Can I check financing first?

Yes. Payment options can be reviewed through Acorn Finance before choosing the final project scope.
