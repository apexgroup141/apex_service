Room-by-room comfort without tearing open the whole house.

Ductless mini-splits are a strong answer when one area never feels right, when an addition needs its own zone, or when the home does not have ducts. We plan head placement, line-set routing, electrical needs and drainage before install day.

  • Single-zone mini-split installation
  • Multi-zone ductless heat pump systems
  • Mini-split replacement and repair
  • Drainage, refrigerant, sensor and electrical troubleshooting
  • Wall, floor and ceiling cassette placement planning

Best uses

ADUs, additions, bedrooms over garages, workshops, finished basements and homes where ducts are not practical.

Homeowner benefit

Quiet operation, efficient heating and cooling, and independent control for the rooms that need it most.
