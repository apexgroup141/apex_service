What we handle

Designed for the home, not guessed from the old unit.

Heat pumps work best when equipment, ducts, controls and airflow are treated as one system. We inspect the existing setup, explain whether fully electric or hybrid makes sense, then install and commission the system cleanly.

  • Ducted heat pump installation
  • Heat pump replacement for aging AC or furnace systems
  • Hybrid heat pump plus gas furnace setups
  • Electric resistance or oil-to-heat-pump conversion planning
  • Load calculation, controls setup and final performance testing

Homes with high bills, aging central AC, electric baseboard heat, oil heat, or uneven comfort between seasons.
