Why is my AC running but not cooling?

Common causes include airflow restrictions, dirty coils, electrical parts, refrigerant issues or aging equipment.

Should I replace AC with a heat pump?

Sometimes. A heat pump may provide both heating and cooling, but the home needs to be reviewed first.
