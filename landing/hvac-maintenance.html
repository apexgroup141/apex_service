Quick answers

Before you request an estimate.

How often should HVAC be maintained?

Most systems should be checked seasonally or at least once a year, depending on age, usage and equipment type.

Can maintenance prevent breakdowns?

It can catch many common issues early, especially airflow, coil, filter, electrical and control problems.
